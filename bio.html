Exam Strategy & Priorities

Curated based on 2023 & 2024 Comprehensive Exams + Course Handout.

🔥 Top 5 Must-Knows

  • DNA Profiling & Restriction Enzymes: Mapping EcoRI/BamHI sites, STRs, sticky vs blunt ends (Pg 220-227).
  • Cellular Respiration vs Photosynthesis: ATP/NADH yields, enzyme inhibition, Calvin cycle tracking (Pg 94-116).
  • Gametogenesis & Cytoplasm: Unequal division in oogenesis, polar bodies vs spermatogenesis (Pg 344).
  • Neural Signaling: Action potentials, Na+/K+ channels, resting potential graph mapping (Pg 364-367).
  • The Nephron: Filtration, reabsorption, secretion. Why glucose in urine = failure (Pg 256-258).

🐴 The "Dark Horses"

(In handout syllabus but highly under-tested in PYQs)

  • Bioinformatics & Genomics: Whole-genome shotgun method, BLAST concepts (Pg 228).
  • Stem Cells: Embryonic vs Adult, therapeutic cloning vs SCNT (Pg 208).
  • Anaerobic Fermentation: Lactic acid vs alcoholic pathways, exact ATP yields (Pg 101).
  • The Lymphatic System: Interstitial fluid, lymph nodes, swelling mechanism (Pg 308).
  • Cancer Genetics: Proto-oncogenes to oncogenes, p53 tumor-suppressor (Pg 208-212).

🧪 Guaranteed Lab Questions

(Directly from Handout Part II Practical List)

  • Exp 2 (Lowry's Method): Protein quantification, reading a standard curve.
  • Exp 4 (Plasmolysis): Osmosis, hypertonic vs hypotonic plant cell behavior.
  • Exp 5 (Transpiration): Stomata function, cobalt chloride test.
  • Exp 7 (Blood Grouping): Antigen-antibody interaction, Rh factor genetics.
  • Exp 9 (Chromatography): Rf value calculation for chlorophyll pigments.

🔬 PYQ Deep-Dive Analysis

Understanding *how* BITS formulates questions based on the textbook.

🧬 2024 Comprehensive Exam

  • Q: Puncturing artificial neuron (Dendrite vs Axon vs Node of Ranvier).
    Book Mapping: Pg 364-365. Tests understanding of signal propagation and myelin sheath gaps.
  • Q: Deep breathing / Pranayama physiological mechanism.
    Book Mapping: Pg 370 (Autonomic Nervous System). Tests application of Parasympathetic (Rest & Digest) vs Sympathetic.
  • Q: Sandwich digestion flow (bread, cheese, egg white).
    Book Mapping: Pg 264-272. Tests ability to map starch (amylase), protein (pepsin), and fats (lipase) through the alimentary canal.
  • Q: PCR mutation detection (3'-AA-5' to 3'-GC-5').
    Book Mapping: Pg 225. Tests primer design concepts and understanding of 5' to 3' directionality.
  • Q: Hair straightening biochemical concept.
    Book Mapping: Pg 45-48. Tests protein structure (primary, secondary, tertiary) and denaturation via heat/chemicals breaking hydrogen/disulfide bonds.

🧪 2023 Comprehensive Exam

  • Q: Polythene bags vs Paper bags in cow digestion.
    Book Mapping: Pg 42 (Cellulose). Tests knowledge of ruminant digestion and symbiotic bacteria that break down cellulose (paper) but not synthetic polymers (plastic).
  • Q: Washing soap "oil stains" (Fatty acids).
    Book Mapping: Pg 43 (Lipids). Tests saponification and the hydrophobic/hydrophilic nature of lipid molecules.
  • Q: Microtubules & Taxol/Colchicine in M-Phase.
    Book Mapping: Pg 126-128. Tests understanding of mitotic spindle formation and chromosome separation in Metaphase/Anaphase.
  • Q: DNA Fingerprinting & Restriction Maps.
    Book Mapping: Pg 220, 226. Requires drawing a circular plasmid map based on given kilobase (kb) band sizes from a gel.
  • Q: SCNT procedure & Electrical shock.
    Book Mapping: Pg 206. Tests understanding of Somatic Cell Nuclear Transfer (cloning Dolly the sheep) and how shock simulates fertilization to start cleavage.
